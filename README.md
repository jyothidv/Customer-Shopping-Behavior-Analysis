# Customer Shopping Behavior Analysis

## Project Overview

Customer Shopping Behavior Analysis is an end-to-end data analytics project that examines customer purchasing patterns using Python, SQL, and Power BI. The project focuses on understanding customer demographics, spending habits, subscription behavior, product preferences, and purchase trends to generate actionable business insights.

The analysis was performed on a dataset containing 3,900 customer purchase records across multiple product categories. The findings help businesses improve customer engagement, optimize marketing strategies, and drive revenue growth.

---

## Business Problem Statement

A leading retail company wanted to better understand customer shopping behavior to improve sales, customer satisfaction, and long-term customer loyalty.

The management team observed changing purchasing patterns across customer demographics, product categories, and sales channels. They wanted to identify which factors such as discounts, product reviews, seasons, subscriptions, and purchasing frequency influence customer decisions and repeat purchases.

### Business Question

**How can the company leverage consumer shopping data to identify trends, improve customer engagement, and optimize marketing and product strategies?**

## Data Preparation & Cleaning (Python)

The dataset was cleaned and transformed using Python before analysis.

### Tasks Performed

* Imported and explored the dataset using Pandas.
* Conducted data quality assessment.
* Checked for missing values.
* Imputed missing values in the Review Rating column using category-wise median values.
* Standardized column names using snake_case formatting.
* Created new features:

  * Age Group
  * Purchase Frequency Days
* Validated data consistency across columns.
* Removed redundant fields after verification.
* Connected Python to MySQL and loaded cleaned data into the database.

---

### SQL Business Analysis

#### 1. Revenue by Gender

Analyzed total revenue generated by male and female customers to understand spending patterns across genders.

#### 2. High-Spending Discount Users

Identified customers who utilized discounts while still spending above the average purchase amount.

#### 3. Top 5 Products by Rating

Determined the products with the highest average customer review ratings.

#### 4. Shipping Type Comparison

Compared average purchase amounts between Standard and Express shipping methods.

#### 5. Subscribers vs Non-Subscribers Analysis

Evaluated differences in average spending and total revenue between subscribed and non-subscribed customers.

#### 6. Discount-Dependent Products

Identified the top five products with the highest percentage of purchases made using discounts.

#### 7. Customer Segmentation

Classified customers into:

* New Customers
* Returning Customers
* Loyal Customers

based on their purchase history.

#### 8. Repeat Buyers and Subscription Relationship

Analyzed whether customers with more than five previous purchases were more likely to subscribe.

#### 9. Top 3 Products per Category

Ranked and identified the three most purchased products within each product category.

#### 10. Revenue by Age Group

Calculated total revenue contribution from different age groups to identify high-value customer segments.
